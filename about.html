Welcome to the British Sub Aqua Club Hull Branch!

Our club was established in 1954 by a small band of adventurous divers with a common aim — just to dive!
It was then known as the Hull Frogmen's Club, eventually becoming Branch 14 of the British Sub-Aqua Club.
We now have around 70 members, including two of the founders; one of whom, now in his 80s, is still diving.
