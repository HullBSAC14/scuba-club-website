Welcome to BSAC 14

​If all you want to do is dive, then you have come to the right place!

Club Meetings are held every Tuesday evening at 8pm, at the Ship Inn (Beverley Rd, Dunswell, Hull HU6 0AJ). Join us for a drink and find out more.

Contact us on hullbsac14@gmail.com for any queries.
​
Find us on Facebook for latest updates: Hull BSAC Divers

Check out British Sub-Aqua Club (BSAC) to see what the our way of diving is all about.
A club membership includes:
  • Access to the club compressor house for free air fills and subsidised mixed gas fills (nitrox and trimix).
  • Access to the club boat (Sea Eagle - a 5.8 m Humber Atlantis RIB, complete with radios, oxygen administration kit & GPS)
  • Access to member-organised national and international dive trips
  • BSAC certified training and skills progression from Ocean Diver to Advanced Diver
  • Dive refreshers
  • Cross-over training from other agencies
  • Club meals and socials​
