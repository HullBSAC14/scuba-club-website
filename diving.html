The North Sea is on our doorstep

During the summer months, we dive the North Sea. Club members regularly launch from Bridlington, Hornsea and Withernsea.
You can expect to dive on dozens of life-encrusted wrecks or take part in scenic dives along the East Yorkshire coastline.

Further afield

UK dive sites well known to club members include:
  • Oban and the Sound of Mull
  • St Abbs
  • Scapa Flow
  • Farne Islands
  • West Cornwall (Porthkerris, Penzance and Sennen)
Past international dive trips include:
  • The Red Sea (Egypt)
  • Malta
  • Bali, Indonesia
  • Truk Lagoon, Micronesia
