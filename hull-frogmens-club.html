Hull Frogmen's Club

Our club owes its roots to the "Hull Frogmen's Club". ​Below are some photographs from Warren Stevenson and Garth Daddy showing some Frogmen's club members and possibly the club's first dive boat in the late 1950s / early 60s. Warren along with Reg Thompson, Brian "Chick" Verity, Garth Daddy are the founding members of the club. They dived with modified gas regulators and any old cylinder they could find!
