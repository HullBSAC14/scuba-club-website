Welcome to BSAC 14

If all you want to do is dive, then you have come to the right place!

Club Meetings are held every Tuesday evening at 8pm, at the Ship Inn (Beverley Rd, Dunswell, Hull HU6 0AJ).

Contact us: hullbsac14@gmail.com

Find us on Facebook: Hull BSAC Divers

A club membership includes:
  • Free air fills and subsidised mixed gas fills
  • Access to the club boat (Sea Eagle RIB)
  • National and international dive trips
  • BSAC-certified training
  • Social events and meals
